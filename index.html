Important

Information that must be filled-in in the PC Usage Record Form are as below :

a. Full Name
b. Student Number / Staff Number
c. PC Number
d. Date of PC Usage
e. Time of PC Usage
f. Subject / Reason of PC Usage
g. Suggestion / Complaint for the PC Number (Optional)

Please make sure all of these information is filled-in before submitting the PC Usage Record Form.

Also make sure all of the PC equipment is complete and the PC is also operating normally.

If there are any incomplete PC equipment or PC operate abnormally, please filled-in the Suggestion / Complaint for that particular PC Number.

Contact

More information regarding PC Usage Record Form for Makmal FHY1, feel free to contact laboratory supervisor Ts. Hanis


The PC Usage Record Form must be filled-in each time the PC at Makmal FHY1 is been used by anyone either a Student, Staff or a Guest
