Important

Information that must be filled-in in the PC Usage Log Form everytime the PC at Makmal FHY1 is been used are as below :

a. Full Name
b. IC Number
c. PC Number
d. Date of PC Usage
e. Time of PC Usage
f. Subject / Reason of PC Usage
g. Suggestion / Complaint for the PC Number (Optional)

Please make sure all of these information is been filled-in before submitting the PC Usage Log Form.

Also make sure all of the PC equipment is complete and the PC is also operating normally.

If there are any incomplete PC equipment or any PC operate abnormally, please filled-in the Suggestion / Complaint for that particular PC Number.
